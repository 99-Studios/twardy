# Twardy
___
The 99 Studios Classics series is a collection of lost, deleted, and unseen games by 99 Studios. Twardy is an accidentally deleted project that never got past its alpha stage. Thanks to the 99 Studios Classics series, you can now play it.

***The following text is the description of the original Twardy game page.***

> Intr[o]ducing WorldStudios[99 Studios]'s first ever farming RPG game! In twardy, you can farm crops to earn xp and survive zombies. There are many seeds that you can get.

Instructions:
=============

-   Move with WASD or arrow keys
-   Walk into seeds to grab them
-   Right-click on farmland with a seed in your hand to plant a seed
-   Right-click on farmland with planted crops to collect the item
-   Right-click off of farmland to drop seeds
-   Left-click on an inventory slot to place your item in it
-   Left-click to punch
-   If you walk into a seed with a seed in your hand, the seed you picked up will replace your current seed
